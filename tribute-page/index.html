Ai Weiwei

Chinese contemporary artist and activist

Timeline of his life

1957: Ai Weiwei is born in China

1957: His family is exiled by communist officials

1976: Allowed to return back to Beijing

1978: Enrolled at the Beijing Film Academy

1981: Moved to the United States

1981: Attends Parsons School of Design

1993: Returned to Beijing

1999: Built his own studio complex

2005: Invited to write a blog

2009: Blog shut down by officials

2009: Assaulted by police while supporting an activist

2010: Installation of 100 million "sunflower seeds" at Tate Modern

2010: His apartment is scheduled to be demolished by officials

2011: Detained for "tax evasion"

2017: Premeired Documentary Human Flow

2018: Studio demolished again by authorities

2020: Released Coronation, a documentary about China's response to the virus
